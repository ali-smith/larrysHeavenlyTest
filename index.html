And God said, "Let there be Larry's."

And there was sauce. God tasted the sauce, and it was good. And God called the sauce Larry’s Heavenly Barbecue Sauce, D’Sauce for All Seasons.

On the second day, God said, “Let there be a firmament in the midst of all food, and let it divide the delicious food from the boring food.” And God called the firmament Larry’s Heavenly Sauces, and they were good.

On the third day, God said, “Let the earth put forth organic herbs, spices and other non-GMO ingredients for wholesome consumption.” And God experimented with them in his test kitchen, and they were good.

On the fourth day, God said, “Let there be beacons of flavor in the firmament of Larry’s Heavenly Sauces, for all seasons and for all dishes.” And God set them in the firmament to give culinary guidance and to rule over the spicy and the tangy, the salty and the sweet. And they were good.

On the fifth day, God said, “Let the waters swarm with farm-raised salmon and other sustainable seafood. And may humanely-raised chickens, free of hormones, roam the land.” And God blessed them, saying, “Be fruitful, and multiply, so that we may always have fish and poultry for Larry’s Heavenly Sauces, because they are good.”

On the sixth day, God said, “Let the earth bring forth grass-fed cows, meatloaf, and vegetables from the farmers’ market,” and it was so. And God put Larry’s Heavenly Sauces on everything that creepeth from or upon the ground, and it was good.

On the seventh day, God blessed the day, and hallowed it, because that in it he created beer and wine, which pair nicely with Larry’s Heavenly Sauces. And they are good.
