And God said, “Let there be Larry’s.”

And there was sauce. God tasted the sauce, and it was good. And God called the sauce Larry’s Heavenly Barbecue Sauce, D’Sauce for All Seasons.

On the second day, God said, “Let there be a firmament in the midst of all food, and let it divide the delicious food from the boring food.” And God called the firmament Larry’s Heavenly Sauces, and they were good.

On the third day, God said, “Let the earth put forth organic herbs and spices for wholesome consumption.” And God put them in Larry’s Heavenly Sauces, and they were good.

On the fourth day, God said, “Let Larry’s Heavenly Sauces shine light upon all the dishes, and govern all the food for all the seasons.” And that was good.

On the fifth day, God said, “Let land and water swarm with humanely-raised poultry and sustainable seafood.” And God blessed them, saying, “Be fruitful, and multiply, so that we may always have fish and poultry for Larry’s Heavenly Sauces, because they are good.”

On the sixth day, God said, “Let the earth bring forth grass-fed cows, meatloaf, and vegetables from the farmers’ market,” and it was so. And God put Larry’s Heavenly Sauces on everything that creepeth from and upon the ground, and it was good.

On the seventh day, God blessed the day, and hallowed it, because that in it he created beer and wine, which pair nicely with Larry’s Heavenly Sauces. And they are good.
